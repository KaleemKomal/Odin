Crookie Recipe


Description

Can satisfy any sweet tooth, this is the quick and easy dessert everyone loves


Ingredients

  • Croissants
  • Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

Instructions

  1. Slice croissants
  2. Cook Chips in deep frying pan
  3. Place the cookie dough into the croissants
  4. Cook in the oven, till cookie dough is lightly browned
