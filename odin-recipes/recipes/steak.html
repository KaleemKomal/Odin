Steak and Chips Recipe


Description

This is a beautifully prepared sirloin steak.

With a side of russet potatoes, double fried to perfection.

Finished off with an elegant garnishing of parsely.


Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Parsely
  • Russet Potatoes
  • Canola Oil

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut potatoes
  2. Cook Chips in deep frying pan
  3. Remove chips when partially cooked
  4. Put in chips for second round of frying
  5. Season steak with salt and pepper
  6. Pour canola oil into pan till smoking hot
  7. Place Steak in the pan, for around 4 minutes
  8. Flip the steak
  9. Put butter, garlic and thyme into the pan
  10. Tilt the pan and baste the steak in the buttery goodness
  11. Remove steak and chips from their respective pans and allow time to rest
  12. Plate and garnish with parsely trimmings
