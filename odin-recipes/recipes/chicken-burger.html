Chicken Burger Recipe


Description

Crispy Crunchy delight.


Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Seasonings
  • Egg
  • Seasoned Chicken Flour
  • Oil
  • Brioche Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Gherkins
  • Condiments Of Choice

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breast
  2. Crack egg and mix with chicken
  3. Coat the chicken in the seaasoned chicken flour
  4. Fryin the oil
  5. Toast buns
  6. Plate burger with lettuce, gherkins, chicken and condiments of choice
